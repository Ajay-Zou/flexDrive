The flexDrive is an implant for chronic extracellular electrophysiology.
The design of the flexDrive is released under the The TAPR Open Hardware License
Get the latest revision of the design source files at http://github.com/open-ephys/flexDrive

The spring design files in this repository are designed for use with wire EDM with a kerf width of 13mil, using any other process requires adapting at least the screw opening in the spring. 

Editing the sldprt of DXF files requires Dassault Systems Solidworks (http://www.solidworks.com/)

Voigts J, Siegle JH, Pritchett DL and Moore CI (2013). 
The flexDrive: An ultra-light implant for optical control and highly parallel chronic recording of neuronal ensembles in freely moving mice. 
Front. Syst. Neurosci. 7:8. doi: 10.3389/fnsys.2013.00008
